Life of Gautam Buddha

People have been following the Buddha's road to freedom for almost 2,500 years. The life tale of the Buddha has inspired and motivated countless others for an equally long time.

The Buddha's life narrative is a tribute to his almost superhuman efforts, his seemingly endless patience, and his profound love and compassion, which inspired him to teach others about this way.

We can follow the path of the Buddha, as long as we are willing to make the necessary effort. As the Buddha said:
“You yourselves must strive, the Buddhas only point the way”
